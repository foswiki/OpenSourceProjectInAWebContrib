Who is in charge to find more information on the Question, for AssignedQuestions.

His duty is to then change the Question status to either:
   * AnsweredQuestions if he found the answer
   * ClosedUnanswered if he found there could be no relevant answer
   * AskedQuestions back to drawing board, if he couldn't find a solution, but there still may be one
